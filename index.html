Mamma-Mia: 5 Stars!

Mamma-Mia, a vibrant movie about family-discovery, is the perfect movie for countless occasions! Whether you're looking for a feel-good movie, an upbeat movie, or a date-night movie, Mamma Mia is the perfect choice!

What Makes It Great

Music: The movie recreates many of ABBA's most famous songs, resulting in a plot which is enhanced by various upbeat, emotional, and comedic songs.

Setting: Taking place in a small island off of Greece, the mvoie's setting features blue seas, beautiful Greek architecture, and plenty of scenic shots.

Humor: It's tried and true that this movie is perfect for all ages. Innuendos will surely entertain those old enough to understand them, while leaving those too young innocent but still enamored by the vibrant sounds and sights of the movie.

Cast: Meryl Streep, Amanda Seyfried, Christine Baranski, Julie Walters, Stellan Skarsgard, Colin Firth, Pierce Brosnan, and Dominic Cooper. What more needs to be said?
